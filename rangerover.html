RangeRover SV

A modern and sophisticated interior, underpinned by its impeccable reductive nature and intuitive approach to relevant technology.

Choose from a range of graded leathers, all with the luxury and refinement you would expect. Available in four colours.

Non-leather alternatives of KvadratTM textile and UltrafabricsTM provide a soft material with a modern look.

Personalise your interior with a selection of fine veneers and a range of materials and finishes.

Range Rovers offer a variety of engine options, including powerful V8s, efficient 6-cylinder engines, and hybrid powertrains. The 2025 Range Rover, for example, features a 606 HP twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 and a 542-horsepower turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 plug-in hybrid. Additionally, the Range Rover SV model is available with a

example of engines

v8 engine

4.4-liter V8 (e.g., in Range Rover Sport) and 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 (e.g., in Range Rover Sport SV).
